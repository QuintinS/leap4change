LEAP4Change believes success is easier when children can make the most of their education, and making the most of their education is easier when they’ve developed basic skills and knowledge.

What We Do

Leap4Change is a company that links volunteers with the people who need them.

We help children to achieve their developmental goals by implementing programmes to assist them across South Africa. We connect passionate and dedicated volunteers with the children and schools who need them most. This is volunteering in its truest form.

Are you interested in volunteering? We will provide you with all the training you need to be able to work with children using the booster programme, a proven system for nurturing young minds.

How We Do It

We work with schools, Early Childhood Development centres and Daycare centres throughout the Western Cape who need volunteers to help the children learn using the education booster programme. Leap4Change connects you, the volunteer, with our partners and place you in a position where you can make a difference. As part of our programme, we will give you the appropriate training for what you’ll be doing in the time that you have available.

Your contribution could range from developing fine motor skills of toddlers through play, or teaching children to count and read, to encouraging teamwork through sport.

Are you a graduate? School leaver? Do you love children? Believe in education? We will match your skills and interests to a project which is a perfect match for you.
