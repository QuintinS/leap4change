Organisations

There are many of us working toward improving education in South Africa and if we work together we can make our collective goal a reality.

The booster programme has shown spectacular results in a short time frame. Assessments have shown phenomenal improvements in the understanding and retention of the foundation concepts in children with published results showing improvements of 70 - 90% in Children grade R - 1.

By using volunteers the programme is easily implemented and does not place additional stress on teacher or learner. The programme has been structured in such a way that the activities are easily be incorporated into the daily schedule yet still maintain their effectiveness.

We offer training and support to Educators, parents, aunties, uncles, grannies and grandads using the booster programme. If you are involved in a Creche or a Daycare Centre? An NPO or NGO working with young children? Or a school.

Anyone who wants to see the children in their care succeed can implement this programme. We can provide you with the basic training, materials and ongoing support to give the children in your care a head start in their education or assist them to catch up so that they can cope with the CAPS curriculum.

If need a technical volunteer with a special skill or you would like to start the programme in your home, centre or school please contact us. We can assist you by placing volunteers to assist with administration, web design, M&E, photography etc. or to implement the booster programmes so that the children in your care can receive the quality foundation that should be theirs.
