Volunteer Opportunities

Casual Volunteers

You do not have to have a qualification to make a real difference in the life and education of our children. Only an interest in children's education, a willingness to learn and drive to make a difference. As a casual volunteer you are a valuable member of the team. We will train you to teach the children the foundational concepts they need to succeed. You will be hands on providing the support, the care, the attention and the education that some children would otherwise not receive.

Does this sound like you? Volunteer Today!

Technical Volunteers

The organisations LEAP4Change work with often need volunteers with special skills. Due to a lack of resources having a volunteer with a technical / academic skill is invaluable. If you have experience or a qualification in a specific area like Monitoring and Evaluation, Graphic design, Occupational therapy, Arts, Sport or Teaching we would love to hear from you. Your mornings will be spent working with the children in the classrooms and afternoons assisting the organisation in your area of expertise. An excellent way to gain experience while benefiting others.

Does this sound like you? Volunteer Today!
