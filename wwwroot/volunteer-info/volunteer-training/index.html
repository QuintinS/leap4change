Volunteer Training

Training and orientation is scheduled for the first week after your arrival. During these training sessions we will cover everything you need to know about the education programme, how to use the materials and games, and what will be expected of you at your project.

The training is fun and interactive, and everyone has a chance to teach and experience the programme from the child's perspective. The training is non-academic and you do not need to have any previous experience, only an outgoing personality, willingness to learn and a passion for making a difference.

We are here for you every step of the way to help and guide you. You receive full ongoing training and support, so that together we ensure that each child receives the gift of education.

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.

Nelson Mandela
