Additional Activities

The Western Cape is the most popular province for Tourists in South Africa. It offers exceptional natural beauty and outdoor activities, cultural diversity, excellent wines and fantastic cuisine.

Over the weekends when you are not working with the children or before and after your projects we can organise a wide range of tours, day trips and activities. From winetasting to hiking up the famous Table Mountain there are myriad of activities to suit each of your personal tastes.

Some activities that can be arranged:

  • Tours - 2 to 5 day tours from Cape Town around the Peninsula to the West Coast or Garden Route and Addo Elephant park

  • Surfing Lessons

  • Kite boarding

  • Sand boarding

  • Scuba diving

  • Mountain walks and hikes

  • Shark cage diving

  • Wine tasting

  • Traditional cooking lessons in Cape Malay Cuisine

  • Live sporting events

  • Theatre

  • Music concerts

Whatever your interests are, we will assist you with all necessary arrangements to ensure that your free time is as fun and rewarding as the work you do at your projects.
